LockSmith Lizzie Long Arm Quilting Machine

Exceeding Expectations

LockSmith Lizzie is your affordable entry-level machine that exceeds expectations! The 3-year electronics and lifetime casting warranty, oil reservoir, built-in bobbin winder, largest capacity bobbin, all new direct drive motor, needle up/down and with front and rear curved handles make the all new S22 LockSmith Lizzie Long Arm your best choice when considering an affordable machine that is anything but ordinary.

Affordability Meets Quality

Whether you’re just starting out or you need a long arm quilting machine that’s truly your partner, LockSmith Lizzie Long Arm is the machine that gives it all! Amazing, precise stitches with tons of features at an entry level price.


iPad App Included

Connect your LockSmith Lizzie to your iPad (iOS version 9.0 ir higher) wirelessly through bluetooth technology and download the app on the app store to access all the controls and features of your machine.

Auto Stitch

LockSmith Lizzie features soft-touch button controls on each handlebar with that is compatible with your iPad (iOS version 9.0 ir higher). When you select the Auto Stich mode from your Lizzie App on your iPad, your machine coordinates with your movements. As you move the machine it sews, when you stop it stops with you. With stitch regulation so precise, and stitching as small as 24 stitches per inch, the Auto Stitch allows for even the finest micro-stippling.

Auto Needle Positioner

LockSmith Lizzie Long Arm has the ability to stop in the needle up or needle down position, minimizing bent or broken needles and allowing free movement of the machine.

Built-in Bobbin Winder w/Large Bobbin

LockSmith Lizzie Long Arm offers a built-in bobbin winder that winds while you quilt, so your next bobbin is always full and ready. It also features the largest available bobbin capacity, the M-size bobbin

Curved Handlebars

LockSmith Lizzie Long Arm has Curved front and rear curved handlebars allowing for easy movement of the machine and help to ease tension and tired muscles, making quilting easier.

Direct Drive Motor

LockSmith Lizzie Long Arm provides a more responsive and accurate stitch with less wear on the internal parts of your machine thanks to the all new direct drive motor.

Oil Reservoir

LockSmith Lizzie Long Arm requires little maintenance. The oil is self-contained so you no longer have to worry about oil on your quilts or oiling the hook with every bobbin change.
