Quilter's Pro Long Arm Machine

Exceeding Expectations

Quilter's Pro is your affordable entry-level machine that exceeds expectations! The 1-year warranty, oil reservoir, built-in bobbin winder, largest capacity bobbin, all new direct drive motor, needle up/down and adjustable front and rear curved handles all make the Quilter's Pro Long Arm your best choice when considering an affordable machine that is anything but ordinary.

Affordability Meets Quality

Whether you’re just starting out or you need a long arm quilting machine that’s truly your partner, Quilter's Pro Long Arm is the machine that gives it all! Amazing, precise stitches with tons of features at an entry level price.

7.9" LED iPad Mini Included FREE! Connect your Quilter's Pro long arm to the included iPad Mini wirelessly through bluetooth technology and download the app on the app store to access all the controls and features of your machine.
Regulated Stitch Quilter's Pro Long Arm features soft-touch button controls on each handle with an LCD display. When you select the Regulated Stitch mode on the LCD screen, your machine coordinates with your movements; as you move the machine it sews, when you stop it stops with you. With stitch regulation so precise, and stitching as small as 22 stitches per inch, the Regulated Stitch allows for even the finest micro-stippling.
Auto Needle Positioner Quilter's Pro Long Arm has the ability to stop in the needle up or needle down position, minimizing bent or broken needles and allowing free movement of the machine.

Built-in Bobbin Winder w/Large Bobbin Quilter's Pro Long Arm offers a built-in bobbin winder that winds while you quilt, so your next bobbin is always full and ready. It also features the largest available bobbin capacity, the M-size bobbin
Adjustable Curved Handlebars Quilter's Pro Long Arm has adjustable front and rear curved handlebars allowing for easy movement of the machine and help to ease tension and tired muscles, making quilting easier.
Direct Drive Motor Quilter's Pro Long Arm provides a more responsive and accurate stitch with less wear on the internal parts of your machine thanks to the all new direct drive motor.
